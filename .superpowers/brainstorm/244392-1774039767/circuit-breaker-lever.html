Design Notes

All three will be rendered as compact inline elements (~30-40px wide) positioned at the right edge of the maintenance banner. The mockups above are enlarged for detail. Click animation will be smooth CSS transitions (0.3-0.5s). Each will have a subtle metallic sheen and period-appropriate textures.
