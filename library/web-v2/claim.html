Email Link

Sign In via Email

Each time you sign in, we send a link to your email. Click the link and you're in — no codes or apps needed.

  • Nothing to install — just check your email
  • Works on any device with email access
  • No codes to type or apps to set up
Not sure which to pick? If you have a modern phone or computer, Use Your Face, Fingerprint, or PIN is the easiest choice. If you just want an email link each time, choose Sign In via Email.
Detailed comparison:
  • Face / Fingerprint / PIN: Easiest daily use. Sign in the way you unlock your phone. Best for most people.
  • Physical Key: A small device you plug in or tap. Best if you already own one (like a YubiKey).
  • Free Phone App: Type a 6-digit number from your phone each time. Works everywhere.
  • Email Link: We email you a sign-in link each time. Simplest option — nothing to install or remember.
Can I change my mind later?

Yes — you can change your sign-in method anytime in your profile settings.

How does this work?

Your phone or computer already knows how to recognize you — with your face, fingerprint, or a PIN you type. This option uses that same recognition to sign you in. No extra app needed.

What happens:
  1. You set it up once (right now)
  2. Next time you sign in, your device asks you to unlock it (face, fingerprint, or PIN)
  3. That's it — you're in!
Good to know:
  • Only works on the device where you set it up
  • If you lose your device, you can use emergency backup codes
Best for: Most people! Especially if you'll use the same phone or computer each time.
What is a physical key?

It's a small device (like a USB stick or keychain fob) that you plug into your computer or tap against your phone. Popular brands: YubiKey, Google Titan.

What happens:
  1. You plug in or tap your key
  2. Touch the key when it blinks
  3. You're signed in!
Good to know:
  • You need to buy one ($25–50)
  • You must carry it with you
  • Works on any device — just bring your key
Best for: People who already own a YubiKey or similar device.
How does this work?

You install a free app on your phone. The app shows a 6-digit number that changes every 30 seconds. When you sign in, you open the app and type that number.

What happens:
  1. Download a free app (we'll help you pick one)
  2. Point your phone's camera at a special code on screen
  3. Each time you sign in, open the app and type the 6 digits you see
Good to know:
  • You need your phone nearby each time you sign in
  • You'll need to type 6 digits each time
  • Works on any computer or device — very flexible
Best for: People who sign in from many different devices, or who want the most flexible option.
How does this work?

When you want to sign in, you enter your username and we send a link to your email. Click the link and you're logged in — no codes, no apps, no passwords.

What happens:
  1. Enter your username on the login page
  2. Check your email for a sign-in link
  3. Click the link — you're in!
Good to know:
  • You need access to your email each time you sign in
  • Links expire after 15 minutes for security
  • Nothing to install or remember
Best for: People who want the simplest possible sign-in and always have email access.
